Evolv functions with the objective of achieving integrated and inclusive development that benefits both the industry and the civil society making each more diverse and vibrant. The link between social progress and business success is becoming increasingly clear and can no longer be undermined. An eco-system based approach is important in identifying gaps and prioritizing action and strategy. This puts society in the center of analysis creating a sustainable environment for new organizations to emerge and grow with the right objectives and allows clusters of organizations with similar targets to develop. Governments, NGOs, companies, and community members must be involved to create an ecosystem of shared value. This coevolve capabilities enable the fundamental themes of spreading growth to disadvantaged sections of society, planning to meet economic needs, strengthening Indian companies both domestically and internationally and fostering a climate of good governance which is open, transparent and transformative.
