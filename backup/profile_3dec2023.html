PROFILE

Evolv™ is a global program of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII), the non-profit powerhouse with 62 offices worldwide, 10 centers of excellence, 9000 corporate members and 300,000+ affiliated entities worldwide. Evolv™ aspires to be the global facilitator for innovators and their startups by connecting them to investors, big enterprise, and government through a myriad of collaborative engagements. Evolv™ provides the right framework for multidimensional business & technology partnerships, presenting the perfect nexus and global playbook. CII’s strategic objective is to build a strong nexus between the world’s most innovative startups that will become drivers for technological leadership, economic growth, and long-term nation building.


As the official Secretariat for 2023 B20 Summit (https://www.b20india2023.org/) and multiple programs it offers, this is a perfect opportunity for innovators, investors, big enterprises, and governments to get involved.
