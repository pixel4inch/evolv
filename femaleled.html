WHAT'S TRENDING

Women nowadays are now making active participation in all walks of society and the global business market is no exception to this. Driven by increasing financial support, number of female entrepreneurs is witnessing a significant increase in both developed and developing economies. Several employees are of opinion that working in a women-led organizations enable them to maintain an appropriate work-life balance. Comfortable workplace environment, equal pay, and job satisfaction are some of the major reasons behind growing popularity of female-led companies. High job satisfaction, along with ample carrier growth opportunities are propelling professionals to join team of female entrepreneurs. In contrast to their male counterparts, companies led by women CEOs are earning significantly higher profitability margins. One of the major reasons behind growing success of female entrepreneurs is their strategy of providing equal opportunities for all employees. This is assisting organizations in addressing requirement of workers from different racial and ethnic backgrounds. Propelled by their abilities to increase revenues, several leading global organizations are now appointing women in various leadership positions. Earlier, majority of financial institution refrained from making investments in companies led by females. However, due to constantly rising return on investment, several globally recognized firms are now investing in women-run organizations. For instance, We-Fi is currently providing substantial funds for female entrepreneurs owning small businesses. Additionally, Goldman Sachs, a leading global financial services company is offering support for various women-led companies. Constantly rising funding opportunities is enabling female entrepreneurs to introduce a wide range of high-end products as well as services. Apart from earning substantial profits, women-led organizations are also offering ample employment opportunities for young people.


Despite growing voices for women empowerment, number of female-owned small and medium enterprises (SMEs) continue to remain at low levels. Widescale prevalence of gender-based discriminatory practices within the society remain one of the reasons behind such a scenario. As a result, women face a lot of challenges in obtaining loans from financial institutions for their business ventures. Men continues to hold leadership position in highly profitable and faster growing sectors such as computers, electronics, as well as transport. In contrast to this, women participation in labor intensive and less-profitable sectors including retail, textile, and garment is exhibiting a robust increase. This remains one of the major reasons behind constantly widening income gap between men and women. As a result of widescale income disparity, the global economy is incurring losses of $170 trillion. In order to overcome these challenges, various countries are devising a wide range of innovative strategies. Under this initiative, they are simplifying process associated with obtaining loans, grants, and funds from banks for female entrepreneurs. In addition, several governments are undertaking procedures for creating efficient women business associations. Apart from this, they are also giving a lot of impetus on developing innovative policies for supporting women entrepreneurs. All these initiatives are expected to assist countries in significantly increasing presence of women in high-growth business segments.


In order to achieve success in today’s highly competitive global business environment, it is important for entrepreneurs to constantly upgrade their skills. In contrast to their male counterparts, there are relatively few globally recognized female business mentors and guides. As a result, they face a lot of challenges in obtaining necessary guidance and advice required for establishing a high-end global organization. Private clubs, golf courses, and shooting ranges are primarily used for conducting business meetings and building social networks. However, majority of women entrepreneurs are either not a member or else barred from entering these high-profile places. As a result, they are required to undergo a strenuous process for creating their own business networks. Nevertheless, advent of women specific networking clubs is offering a safe and comfortable environment for conducting corporate meetings and events. Various female entrepreneurs are availing services offered by social media platforms such as Instagram and Facebook for connecting with their fellow counterparts. Apart from this, constantly increasing women business mentors is also fostering growth for female entrepreneurs. Despite growing participation of women in economic activities, they are yet to acquire leadership roles in global organizations.


It is mandatory for companies to provide long maternity leaves for pregnant women, which affects their overall productivity. As a result, they usually refrain from offering leadership positions to female employees. In order to address this concern, several globally recognized firms are giving a lot of impetus on creating a women-friendly workplace environment. The innovative strategy is assisting females in creating an appropriate balance between their personal and professional lives. Constantly rising number of female-led organizations is expected to contribute £9.5bn as additional revenues in the UK economy. With an intention to improve employee efficiency, various female-owned organizations are now adopting hybrid work modules. The novel mechanism is playing an important role in significantly enhancing employee productivity as well as performance. In order to curtail costs, several female entrepreneurs are now appointing freelancers for IT support, marketing, as well as preparing pay rolls. The innovative strategy is assisting them in curtailing unnecessary costs, thereby improving their overall profitability margins. On the other hand, it is also offering ample opportunities for freelancers to significantly increase their earnings. While women accounted for 38% of the global workforce, 3.5 million mothers lost their jobs in the year 2020. With majority of firms adopting regressive employment practices, number of unemployed females is witnessing a significant increase. To foster economic growth, several countries are now appointing women in executive as well as leadership positions. Apart from this, they are also offering ample financial incentives for female entrepreneurs. In a nutshell, despite few impediments, the global market for women-owned organizations is expected to witness a robust growth.

Despite earning substantial revenues, women-owned enterprises continue to face a lot of difficulties in accessing funds from financial institutions. As a result, male continues to occupy leadership position in various leading global large-sized business enterprises. Owing to insufficient funding, number of female-led SMEs and MSMEs is yet to witness a significant increase. For instance, women currently own only 28% and 38% of overall global MSMEs and microenterprises, respectively. Majority of financial institutions refrain from making venture capital investment in female-led start-ups. As a result, number of women entrepreneurs continue to remain at a low level in various European Union countries. In order to overcome this challenge, several financial institutions are now giving a lot of impetus on appointing female fund managers. Additionally, with an intention of propelling women entrepreneurship, they introduced dedicated venture capital funds and financial instruments. Introduction of various advanced programs such as Centre for the Development of Industrial Technology, Bpifrance, and Bpifrance are enabling women entrepreneurs to easily obtain funds for their business ventures. In order to increase participation of females in science, technology, engineering and mathematics segments, various European Union countries launched skill development programs. This is playing an important role in significantly increasing number of women scientists as well as researchers. Fueled by high growth prospects, various women entrepreneurs are now establishing their own venture capital investment firms. All these positive developments are expected to open new avenues of growth for female entrepreneurs in the European Union.


While COVID-19 pandemic adversely impacted global economic scenario, it opened new avenues of growth for various small and medium sized organizations. Propelled by promising outlook, approximately 5.4 million new organizations were established in the US during 2021. Out of these, majority were owned by women from Black and African American communities. For instance, percentage of business owned by Black and Hispanic women witness a significant increase of 515% and 450%, respectively. Constantly increasing number of female-led organizations are offering ample job opportunities for people. Apart from this, they are also allowing companies to earn over $385 billion as revenues annually. Growing participation of women in various economic sector would enable the US government to create 4 million new jobs. Apart from this, the novel strategy is also expected to the country to garner $1.2 trillion of additional revenue. Fueled by high growth prospect, several females are now entering the highly prospective microbusinesses segment. At present, women currently own 40% of all microbusinesses in the UK, which is significantly higher than 32% in the year 2020. Similarly, share of black and Asian entrepreneurs in the UK also witness a considerable increase to reach 6.6% as well as 11.9%, respectively. Constantly increasing contribution of women in the global economy is propelling governments to undertake steps for removing gender-based discrimination. In order to fulfil this requirement, various countries are now appointing females on various legislative, managerial, and leadership positions. For instance, Togo and Belarus both has highest number of women performing various technical and professional responsibilities. Due to constantly improving global economic scenario, wages for women are exhibiting a marginal increase.

Constantly increasing environment degradation concerns are propelling countries to devise a wide range of novel strategies. Under this initiative, they are propelling companies operating in the transportation, agriculture, and energy generation sectors to use sustainable technologies. Driven by their abilities to curtail CO2 emission levels, demand for eco-friendly production processes is witnessing a robust surge. Growing impetus on using green technologies is propelling large number of female entrepreneurs to enter the highly prospective segment. For instance, women currently account for majority of workforce in the global renewable energy generation sector. With an intention of increasing female participation, governments introduced various innovative policies and guidelines. This remains one of the major reasons behind constantly rising number of women-owned MSMEs. The innovative strategy is playing an important role in reducing gender-based discrimination at workplaces. Growing inclination towards energy sustainability and circular economy is expected to create 100 million new jobs through 2030. Fueled by high growth prospects, various female entrepreneurs are now making a foray into the highly profitable green energy generation sector.
